Outcome of interest

We wish to validate our estimate of the risk of colorectal cancer (crc), so our outcome of interest is time until crc diagnosis.

The outcome was constructed as follows:

  • time until death was calculated as age from study entry to age at death (or censoring) (t2dth - age_ref)
  • time until dx was calculated as age from study entry to age at crc dx. (age_dxsel - age_ref)

  • The survival time for each observation was taken to be the minimum of the two above times.

  • Question: Is the age_dxsel variable the correct timepoint to use for the advanced adenoma cases? Is this the age of dx for adenoma?

  • Question: there are 31 people that had cause of death (dthc) as ‘colorectal’ cancer but were not diagnosed with crc. I count these as a CRC dx case.
